About Our Beer:

We are a pub at heart and pride ourself on great selctions of beer. Our list is constantly rotating as our brewmaster constantly finds new inspirations. We are blessed to have brought on Charlie Weasley as our executive Brewmaster. His travels in Romania give our clientel at The Leaky Cauldron an unique range of tastes and varieties of beers you cannot get anywhere else. We offer a beer of the month and have just started the Beer 12 Club. If you try each beer of the month you will get an engraved mug and a six pack of your favorite beer of the month. We do change our taps often but offer bottles beers as well. If there is a favorite beer and you do not see it, let us know and we will give you a bottle or special order one for you. We aim to keep your beer taste buds happy!
